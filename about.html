About KMZ-packaging

For over three years, we have been the trusted packaging partner for local cafes, restaurants, and food businesses. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, sustainable packaging solutions with the personalized service that our community deserves.

Today, we're proud to take the lessons learned and expand our services across the UK. Our mission remains the same: to make exceptional, eco-friendly packaging accessible to every food business, supporting your growth every step of the way.

A Better Way to Buy Packaging

After years of working in the food industry, we grew tired of seeing great businesses held back by the same old problems. We knew there had to be a better way.

We saw our colleagues struggling with:

  • Minimum order quantities that were too high for small businesses
  • Inflexible suppliers who couldn't adapt to their needs
  • Unpredictable and long lead times
  • Poor, unresponsive customer service

So, three years ago, we founded KMZ-Packaging to be the supplier we wish we'd had. We built our business on a simple promise: to offer flexible ordering, responsive service, and premium-quality packaging at fair prices.

For three years, we've delivered on that promise for our local partners. Now, we're proud to bring that same commitment to businesses nationwide.

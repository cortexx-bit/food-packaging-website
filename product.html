Why Choose Sustainable Bagasse?

Our packaging isn't just effective; it's a responsible choice for your business and the environment. Here's why bagasse is the superior material for modern food service industry.

Rapidly Renewable Resource

Bagasse is the natural fibre left over after sugarcane is harvested. Unlike traditional paper from trees that can take decades to mature, sugarcane is a rapidly renewable resource that is harvested every year. By choosing bagasse, you are selecting a truly sustainable material that turns an agricultural byproduct into a high-quality product, lessening the strain on our planet's forests.

100% Compostable

Unlike plastic which pollutes our environment for centuries, our bagasse packaging is certified to be fully compostable in a matter of months. This process enriches the soil by turning the packaging and any food scraps inside into nutrient-rich compost, creating a truly circular system that leaves no waste behind.

Uncompromised Performance

Our bagasse containers are grease-resistant, cut-resistant, and suitable for a wide range of temperatures. They are fully microwave and freezer safe, and reliably hold their shape with everything from hot, saucy dishes to fresh, crisp salads without leaking or becoming soggy. This ensures a high-quality experience for your customers and protects your brand's reputation for excellence.

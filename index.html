Certifications Quality, From Source to You

We ensure every product meets the highest standards by partnering with suppliers who hold globally recognized certifications. This, combined with our product-specific verifications, guarantees your packaging is safe, sustainable, and reliable.

  • BRCGS Certified Manufacturing: Our packaging is produced in a facility that holds the BRCGS Global Standard for Packaging Materials—the benchmark for food safety required by UK retailers.
  • FSC® Certified Materials: We exclusively use paperboard from suppliers with Forest Stewardship Council® (FSC®) certification, ensuring all materials are from sustainably managed forests.
  • Certified Compostable: Our products are certified to the EN 13432 standard and by TÜV Austria, guaranteeing they will break down completely in home or industrial composting facilities.
  • ISO Certified Supplier: Our supplier's operations are certified to ISO 9001 and ISO 14001, ensuring consistent quality and a commitment to reducing environmental impact.
